Dashboard Reports

RPA that enables BI Solutions make your customers faster, smarter, and wealthier. The Global Business Intelligence (BI) market is anticipated to expand from to $29.48 billion by 2022.

Aiding Business Intelligence by creation of Dashboards using Robotic Process Automation (RPA). 

A CXO level dashboard in 90% less the time

The IT department of a well-known business conglomerate had run into accuracy issues when presenting their data on the monitoring dashboard. Collecting and processing logged data to create reports for the dashboard that had to be completed in a minimum of two hours. Data from four different systems had to be consolidated. It was inevitable that errors would creep up into this manual process. Errors in reporting rendered the entire monitoring useless, however, and made systems susceptible to failure due to erroneous decision-making attributed to wrong data.

1

Bot downloads the raw files generated for the Backup Systems and the Disk Space systems from an email it receives

2

Bot then reads the logs from monitoring web app and network from the shared location

3

Bot consolidates the raw files and log files into a single data processing spreadsheet

4

Referring to business rules, Bot transforms the data as required to be reported

5

Bot processes and formats this data from all four systems to create a CXO-level dashboard

6

The final dashboard report is then automatically sent to the stakeholders as an attachment to email
