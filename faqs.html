Frequently Asked Questions on RPA

We've got answers

1."Business user is the RPA Developer. The platform claims so, Is not that true?”​

RPA Developer is anyone with reasonable software development experience, May be a Business User.

2."Let’s finish this RPA project in 6 months !!”

RPA is not a project but a continuous discipline.

3.RPA lifecycle is all about automating one after other workflow

Nope, It has at least 3 different things to take care of.
• New Workflow Automation​
• Existing Workflow optimization/ Change Request​
• Live Support

4.Too many ADHOC challenges making BOT’s performance unreliable

You did not think through the basics namely ​
• Water Tight business analysis​
• Bot’s infrastructure need ​
• Optimized Code

5."Let’s automate this , it’s quick and Easy”

Not all processes qualify for RPA, the qualification rate is ​less than 30-40% in our experience.

6.All you need is Excel and a Mail to get started in RPA​

Unless you bring in proven project management systems, You are sure to clog the delivery within a short span.
